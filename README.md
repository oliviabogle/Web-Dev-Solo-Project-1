## Business Description

**Studio 22 | SoHo, New York City**

Studio 22 | NYC is a modern Pilates and movement studio located in the heart of SoHo. Designed to cultivate strength, balance, and community, Studio 22 offers reformer-based classes, heated mat sessions, and sculpt-focused workouts tailored to a range of experience levels.

With an elevated athleisure aesthetic and a calming, minimalist atmosphere, the studio blends functional fitness with intentional design. Studio 22 is more than a workout space — it’s a welcoming environment where members build confidence, improve mobility, and connect through movement.

From structured memberships to flexible drop-in classes, the studio provides accessible options for individuals seeking a supportive and energizing fitness experience.
